You're the Writer, Not Your Computer

20 mins

Check out "You're the Writer, Not Your Computer" to learn how to take back control over your writing and use your computer to your advatage. If you are want to build your writing skills, this artcile will show you the top three ways your computer is hindering your abilities and the top three ways you can use your computer to make your writing better. You'll increase your writing skills when you know how to best use the tools available to you.
