Influencer Watch

Check out "Influencer Watch" and hear about the influencers we think are so much like us, but actually are not. Influencers are a new brand of celebrity, and these young people are making absurd amounts of money. Yet they got their start on the social media apps that we use everyday.

Don't Let Media Consume You

Check out "Don't Let Media Consume You" and learn how technology has made media more accessible, and therefore a more constant part of our lives. It's not a bad thing though. We just need to be aware of how much media we consume, so that we know how it is affecting us.

"36 Ways the Web has Changed Us"

Check out the "36 ways the Web has changed us" article published by The Washington Post. This article talks about all the ways the Internet has influenced our lives (spoiler alert: we've stopped doing a lot of things that we used to do).
